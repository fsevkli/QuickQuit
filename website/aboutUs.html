About Us

We are a group of students from Miami University in Oxford, Ohio, U.S.A. We are passionate about technology and want to utilize it to make a positive impact on the world. Our goal for this project is to provide safety to the vulnerable people who need it most.

Our Story

In January 2025, we traveled to Manchester, England, for a study abroad program. There, we learned about the homelessness crisis and explored websites such as Justlife and Lifeshare. The "get me out" button on these websites inspired us to create QuickQuit—aimed at offering users an easy escape and privacy in a time of need.
