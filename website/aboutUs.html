About Us

We are a group of students from Miami University in Oxford, Ohio, U.S.A. We are passionate about technology and want to utilize it to make a positive impact on the world. Our goal for this project is to provide safety to the vulnerable people who need it most.

Our Story

In January 2025, we went on a study abroad to Manchester, England. Here we had the opportunity to learn more about the homelessness crisis facing Manchester while also looking over the websites Justlife and Lifeshare, which provide resources to people in need. An aspect of these websites that stood out to us was the "get me out" button. We had never seen anything like this before...
