About QuickQuit

QuickQuit is a free Chrome Extension made by students at Miami University

What Does QuickQuit Do?

QuickQuit is a free and innovative Chrome extension designed to help users swiftly exit websites with resources to assist escape from domestic violence. With just the click of a button, QuickQuit replaces the URL of the current tab with a random, unrelated URL, making it look like you've navigated to an entirely different site. But that's not allâ€”this powerful tool also rewrites the browser history, replacing any instance of the original website's domain with the new, random URL.

The primary purpose of QuickQuit is to provide a fast and discreet way to leave potentially dangerous or sensitive websites without leaving a trace. It is especially valuable for individuals in precarious situations, where accessing certain websites could put their safety or privacy at risk. Whether someone is seeking critical resources online but needs to protect their digital footprint, QuickQuit ensures that they can exit the site quickly while making it nearly impossible for anyone to discover their online activity.
