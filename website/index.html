What Does QuickQuit Do?

QuickQuit is a free Chrome extension designed to replace the URL of the current tab with a random URL. Along with replacing the URL the extension will also rewrite the history replacing any occurence of of the website's domain name with a random URL link. The purpose of QuickQuit is to create away to quickly leave a website and rewrite the history by simply pressing a button. This makes QuickQuit an effective tool for helping people in dangerous situations accessing your website for resources but for their safety need to make sure no one nows they are accessing these sites.
